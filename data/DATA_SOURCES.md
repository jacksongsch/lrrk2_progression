# Data Sources

Source data are not redistributed. All inputs are available to approved
researchers through the access route listed below.

---

## PPMI

**Access:** Free registration and data use agreement at
[ppmi-info.org](https://www.ppmi-info.org)
(RRID: SCR_006431).

**Data vintage:** Last accessed via the PPMI portal on July 1, 2026.
Longitudinal data collected between July 7, 2010 and June 30, 2026.

### Required variables

**Demographics and genotyping.** Age at enrollment, symptom onset date,
diagnosis date, PD baseline date, sex, self-reported race, enrollment
status, PPMI cohort, and phenoconversion status. *LRRK2* variant calls
(G2019S, R1441G, M1646T) with allele dosage. Monogenic status
for *LRRK2*, *GBA*, *SNCA*, *PRKN*, *PINK1*, *PARK7*, and *VPS35* used
for cohort assignment and exclusions.

**Primary outcome.** MDS-UPDRS Part III item-level scores with
page name, medication state, and treatment status for OFF-state
classification. Subscores derived from Part III items: bradykinesia
(3.4–3.8, 3.9, 3.14), rigidity (3.3a–e), tremor (3.15–3.18), axial
(3.10–3.13), and orofacial (3.1–3.2). Levodopa
equivalent daily dosage (LEDD) at each visit. 

**Secondary outcome.** MDS-UPDRS Part II.

**Exploratory outcomes.** MDS-UPDRS Parts I and IV. MoCA. DAT-SPECT striatal 
binding ratios (caudate and putamen).

**Sensitivity analyses.** α-synuclein seed amplification assay (SAA)
status, baseline visit prioritized.

### Genotyping

*LRRK2* variant calls and genetic exclusions are derived from PPMI 
whole-genome sequencing (Genetics Core, Illumina, GATK Best Practices) and 
the PPMI IU Genetic Consensus document.
